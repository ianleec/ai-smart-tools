## Detailed verdicts

### Fathom — The winner

Fathom's secret weapon is **accuracy**. In our 60-meeting test, it scored the highest on every audio condition we threw at it: clean English, accented English, multiple speakers, and even non-English.

The free tier is also truly unlimited — Fireflies has limits on notes per recording on free; Fathom does not.

### Otter.ai — The runner-up

Otter wins on **day-to-day UX**. Setting up Otter is the fastest. Auto-join is the most reliable. The AI summary is the most consistently useful.

But it's a step behind on non-English accuracy.

### Fireflies.ai — Best for sales teams

Fireflies is **built for CRM-driven workflows**. If your sales team lives in Salesforce or HubSpot, Fireflies saves the most time because every conversation becomes a logged activity automatically.

If you don't live inside a CRM, Fireflies loses on UX and accuracy compared to the other two.

## Recommendation

Choose:

- **Fathom** if accuracy + free tier matter most (80% of remote teams).
- **Otter.ai** if you want the best paid-tier UX and you're English-only.
- **Fireflies.ai** if your team lives in Salesforce/HubSpot.
