## Who Otter.ai is best for

Otter is designed for English-first **Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams** users who want a frictionless, "just works" experience. It's strong for:

- **Founders and freelancers** who run all their work over Zoom
- **Sales teams** that need call transcripts and AI summaries for HubSpot/Salesforce
- **Recruiters** doing 10+ interviews a week

If your meetings are in mixed languages (Mandarin + English, Spanish + English), Fathom is better. If you live inside Salesforce or HubSpot, Fireflies has tighter CRM integrations.

## What I liked

### 1. Setup is genuinely zero-effort
I connected Google Calendar. Within 30 seconds, Otter auto-joined my next 12 calendar events without me installing any plugins or per-meeting bots. Compare that to Fathom (which requires a manual click in some browser extensions) and Fireflies (which requires the Slack bot to be invited), and Otter wins on raw setup time.

### 2. AI summary is genuinely useful — most days
Otter's AI Summary produced a 4-bullet "what happened" digest and an action item list for **38 of 47 meetings** that I used as-is. That's roughly 4-6 minutes saved per meeting.

### 3. Sales-flavored features are real, not fluff
"Ask Otter" lets you type natural-language questions ("What did the prospect say about pricing?") across your entire library of past meetings. Sales reps will find this a productivity lever.

## What I didn't like

### 1. Non-English is mediocre
On three Mandarin meetings (which I ran specifically to test this), Otter averaged 61% accuracy. By comparison, Fathom averaged 84% on the same audio. If your team is multilingual, this matters.

### 2. Free tier is a soft wall
300 minutes/month is roughly 6 hours — it's enough for the first 30 days of testing, but the moment you have back-to-back meetings, you'll bump into the cap and feel pressured to upgrade.

### 3. Editor UX feels dated
The transcript editor, where you fix errors and tag speakers, looks like 2018 software. Fireflies and Fathom both ship a noticeably more modern interface.
