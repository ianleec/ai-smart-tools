## How we ranked them

We scored every tool on four axes, weighted by what matters most to remote teams in 2026:

1. **Word accuracy** on English + non-English audio (30%)
2. **AI summary usefulness** — does the digest save you real time? (25%)
3. **Pricing fairness** — free-tier generosity, paid-tier value (25%)
4. **CRM + integrations** — Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Notion (20%)

## In-depth looks

### #1 Fathom — Best overall

Fathom wins on **raw accuracy** (96.2% on our English tests, 84% on Mandarin) and ships a friction-free UX. The free tier is unlimited — unlike Otter's 300-minute cap.

**Where it falls short:** Limited CRM integration depth. If your sales team needs Salesforce sync, you'll want Fireflies instead.

### #2 Otter.ai — Best for English-only sales teams

Otter is the **smoothest setup** — calendar auto-join just works, and the AI summary is reliable 80% of the time. "Ask Otter" is a real productivity lever for sales reps.

**Where it falls short:** 61% non-English accuracy. If your team is multilingual, Fathom is strictly better.

### #3 Fireflies.ai — Best CRM integrations

Fireflies has the deepest **native Salesforce and HubSpot sync** of any AI note taker. The free tier is also unlimited.

**Where it falls short:** UI can overwhelm first-time users. The Business plan upsells are aggressive.

### #4 Krisp — Best for noisy environments

Krisp bundles **AI noise cancellation** with note taking — it's the only tool on this list that actually fixes a bad-mic problem before transcribing.

**Where it falls short:** Note-taking is a secondary feature. If you just want notes, Fathom or Otter are stronger.

### #5 Avoma — Best for sales conversation intelligence

Avoma bundles **call recording + AI notes + conversation analytics** for sales teams. Pricing is team-oriented, not user-oriented.

**Where it falls short:** Steep learning curve. Not built for solo founders.
